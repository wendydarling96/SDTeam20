We know design.

The purpose of this project is to create a vehicle in which students from the FAMU-FSU College of Engineering design, build, and compete in the ASME Human Powered Vehicle Challenge. The Human Powered Vehicle Challenge is a competition in which students design and assemble an eco-friendly, agile, and safe mode of transportation. The goal of the competition is to find a design for a human powered vehicle that can be used in megacities. This semester, our team is finalizing our design, performing the necessary failure analysis, as well as building our first prototype. We are continually sharing concepts and ideas with faculty and machine shop staff to validate our design and make further improvements. Additionally, we are recruiting younger students from student organizations and classes at the college of engineering that are interested in designing and manufacturing a vehicle.
